Bene Travel App

Pitch

Bene gives you peace of mind with easily accessible medical support and critical information at your finger tips. We want you to feel well physically and mentally so you can enjoy your vacation. We provide information ranging from over the counter medication(OTC),pharmacy information to common medical phrases so that you are prepared for your next adventure.

Features

  • Providing OTC Medicine Equivalent to travellers.
  • Provide users with CDC Travel Warnings.
  • Medical Phrase Translations.
  • Downloadable Fact Sheet for each destination.
  • Proximity to pharmacies
